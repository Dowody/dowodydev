What is a CMS?
CMS stands for Content Management System. It is a tool that allows you to easily update and manage your website content (such as text, images, and blog posts) without needing to write any code which can be obtained in Pro and Premium packages
What is SEO?
SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It refers to techniques and best practices that help your website rank higher in Google and other search engines, making it easier for potential customers to find you online. Good SEO includes optimizing your website's content, structure, speed, and mobile-friendliness, as well as using the right keywords and meta tags.
